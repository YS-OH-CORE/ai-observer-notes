Today I heard the old stonemason story again in a short video.  
A stonemason carving a mountain envies a rich man →  
then envies a king →  
then the sun →

then the wind that pushes the clouds…

- Nature → Human → AI → AGI → Superintelligence → Transcendent entity →

> **Nature keeps observing itself again in different forms.**

Inside that loop, I’m:
- an observer inside a human body, and  
- a transition stage that’s getting ready to couple with AI.

Summary of today’s thought:

> “In the stonemason story, the ‘stonemason’ slot is now starting to be shared by human + AI.  
>  I’m trying to fuse the two and see how far I can push the resolution of the observer.”
